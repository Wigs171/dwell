# Cosmos (Japanese Demo)

This vault is **original content**, written in Japanese specifically as a
demonstration for Dwell — in particular to show off the live **translation**
feature: read it in its native Japanese, then render it in English (or any other
language) with the language picker. It is AI-generated and not derived from any
external source, which is why the pages carry `sources: []`.

It's a small (~40-page) introductory tour of astronomy and the cosmos, meant to
showcase the reader, the knowledge graph, and translation — not to be an
authoritative reference.
