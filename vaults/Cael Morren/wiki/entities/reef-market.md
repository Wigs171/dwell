# Reef Market

## The Market That Floats

Where the tide sits neither high nor low, out on the middle water of [[cael-morren]], the Reef Market gathers. It is no building but a raft of them — hulls lashed to barges, barges to pontoons, a whole quarter of the city that rides the flood instead of drowning in it. Ropes creak, lanterns swing, and the whole floating warren shifts its shape with the water so that no two seasons find it in the same place twice. Buyers step from deck to deck over green channels; a stall that was a bow-length off yesterday may be moored across the market today. The Reef belongs to no tier, and so to every tier.

## Where the Ferrymen Trade

The Reef is the heart-ground of [[the-ferrymen]], the faction that thrives on the flux rather than fighting it. They move goods and people through the changing water, from the high dry tiers down to the flooded lanes, and much of what they carry surfaces first here. Salt, rope, saltfish, tallow, and — clouded and precious, wrapped against the damp — sheets of [[saltglass]] pass hand to hand across these decks. The Ferrymen have no wish to see the tide stilled; a still sea would sink their whole living. Their loyalty is to motion.

## Smuggler's Water

Not all that changes hands at the Reef is honest. Its shifting geography makes it perfect for the quiet trade, and no one works that trade better than [[oren-quill]], the Ferryman who knows the drowned lanes of the older town by heart. Through the Reef he moves what other men cannot — down into the flooded quarters, out past the mark, into the barnacled dark that surfaces only at the low ebb. To the honest trader the market is where the tiers meet and bargain. To Oren Quill it is a doorway to everywhere the water hides.
