# Biology 101 — demo vault

This vault is **original content**, written specifically as a demonstration for
Dwell. It is **not** derived from any external textbook or source — every page
was authored for this project, which is why the pages carry `sources: []` and
make no citations.

It is a deliberately small (~40-page) first-year-biology overview meant to show
off the reader and the knowledge-graph features (steering, voices, missed
connections, quizzes), not to be a complete or authoritative course.
